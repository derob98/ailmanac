**AILmanac** is a free, community-built **almanac for getting the most out of Claude — and any AI**. An almanac is a reference you come back to: complete, plain-spoken, and kept current. That's the goal here.

<Callout type="objectives" items={["Understand what AILmanac is — and what it deliberately is not", "Find the level that fits you: beginner, intermediate, or advanced", "Pick your fastest way in and start reading in under a minute"]} />

## Who this is for

Everyone, on purpose — and we tell you the level of every page so you never feel lost or talked down to:

| Level | This is you if... |
| --- | --- |
| <LevelBadge level="beginner" /> | You've barely used AI and want a guaranteed first win. |
| <LevelBadge level="intermediate" /> | You use Claude already and want noticeably better results. |
| <LevelBadge level="advanced" /> | You build with Claude Code, the API, agents, and MCP. |

## What makes this different

There are excellent resources already (see [the Landscape Map](/docs/start-here/landscape-map)). AILmanac is **not** a link dump and **not** a copy of the official docs. It is:

- **Opinionated** — we tell you the *one recommended way*, then the alternatives.
- **Level-tagged** — every page is badged so you read what's right for you.
- **Template-rich** — [copy-paste starters](/docs/templates) you can use in 30 seconds.
- **Honest about freshness** — facts that change (model names, prices, features) carry a "last verified" date and a link to the source.

## What this is NOT

:::warning The official docs are the source of truth
When AILmanac and the [official Anthropic documentation](https://platform.claude.com/docs) disagree, **the official docs win**. AI tools change fast; we work hard to stay current, but always confirm anything critical against the source we link to.
:::

We also don't store your data, can't see your account, and won't make decisions for you on high-stakes work — see [Responsible Use](/docs/security).
