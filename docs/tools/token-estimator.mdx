<TokenEstimator />

:::tip Why tokens matter
Context limits and pricing are both measured in tokens (≈ ¾ of a word). Trimming what you send cuts both cost and latency. Deep dive in [Tokens, Context & Pricing](/docs/api/tokens-and-pricing).
:::
